ST-TRACS

Teseo Receiver Advanced Control Suite

An Android application dedicated to GPS & Dead Reckoning data analysis

Discover

ST-TRACS is an application developped by 8 students from INSA Rennes' Computer Science department, in collaboration with STMicroelectronics

Client

STMicroelectronics' automotive unit was seeking assistance to address a real need

Requirement

The company wants to test its Dead Reckoning solution, a technology giving the opportunity to continue GPS navigation even when satellite signals are lost.

Solution

The intended solution is an Android application, allowing STMicroelectronics' employees to visualize GPS & Dead Reckoning data retrieved from the tested chip.

Overview

Skyview

  • Visualization of available satellites
  • Orientation
  • Comparison between GPS and Dead Reckoning positions
  • Charts help to visualize differences between the two technologies
  • Information about Dead Reckoning
  • Accurate information for each available satellite
  • Satellites ranked by signal strength (CN0)
  • Classical GPS view
  • General information about GPS positioning (maximal speed, position accuracy, etc.)
  • Visualization of raw data retrieved from the GPS chip
